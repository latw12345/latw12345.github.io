Where Nature Touches Community:
How Green Space Shapes Emotional Support Within Washington’s Uneven Landscape of Income

This map examines how green space accessibility overlaps with the share of adults lacking social and emotional support across Washington State, viewed alongside surrounding median household income. Insets for Seattle–Tacoma, Spokane, and Vancouver highlight local variation within the broader statewide pattern.

Ballot Dropbox Accessibility in King County
Proximity, walkable access, and where high-elderly districts fall within the pattern
How Close Are King County Residents to a Ballot Dropbox?

This map visualizes straight-line distance rings around ballot dropboxes to show how proximity varies across King County.

How Walkable Is Access to Ballot Dropboxes Across King County, and Where Do High-Elderly Districts Fall Within This Pattern?

This map compares district-level distance to the nearest dropbox with the distribution of older adults to highlight potential accessibility gaps.
