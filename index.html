Ballot Dropbox Access (King County)
How Close Are King County Residents to a Ballot Dropbox?

Distance rings around ballot dropbox sites summarize how close residents are to a dropbox across King County.

District-level distance to the nearest dropbox is mapped alongside the share of residents aged 65+ to highlight potential walkability gaps.
